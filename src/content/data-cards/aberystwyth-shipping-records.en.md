## Overview

This dataset results from the transcription of crew accounts and agreements, generally known as crew lists, for vessels registered at the port of Aberystwyth, Cardiganshire, Wales, UK. The records were transcribed from originals held at the National Library of Wales by volunteers working through the NLW Volunteering Programme.

- Creation date: 6 July 2015

- Languages: English

## Source

The Merchant Shipping Act 1835 required all British registered ships of 80 tons or more employed in the coastal trade or fisheries to carry crew agreements and accounts. At the end of each voyage, the master sent these crew lists to the Registrar General of Shipping and Seamen through Mercantile Marine Offices. Home trade crew lists were delivered half-yearly, while voyages outside British coastal waters generated one list per voyage.
