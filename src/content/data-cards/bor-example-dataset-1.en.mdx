# Title: Welsh Book of Rembrance

## About the data

* **What?** - individuals are listed according to regiment and battalion alongside the names of those who might well have died alongside them.
* **Who?** - The National Book of Remembrance was digitised by the National Library of Wales in conjunction with the Welsh Centre for International Affairs as part of the Wales for Peace programme. Over 100 volunteers have been busy transcribing and tagging the names – a digital act of remembrance so that each person can be remembered for generations to come.

### Description

The Welsh National Book of Remembrance for the First World War contains the names of 35,000 servicemen and women, as well as members of Welsh Regiments, who lost their lives in the First World War
